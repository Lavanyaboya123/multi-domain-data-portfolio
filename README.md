## Project 1: Retail – Supermarket Sales Analysis

**Objective**  
Analyze 1,000 supermarket transactions to understand sales patterns, top products, customer behavior, and generate business recommendations.

**Key Findings**
- Total Revenue: $322,966.75  
- Average Transaction: $322.97 | Median: $253.85  
- Top Product Categories: Food & beverages, Fashion accessories, Electronic accessories  
- Customer Insights: Females contribute ~51% of sales; Members and Normal customers nearly equal  
- Payment Methods: Ewallet, Cash, Credit card
